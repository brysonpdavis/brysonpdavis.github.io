A Place Split Between Dimensions

crosshatch is an atmospheric puzzle game about exploring a garden caught between parallel timelines.

Currently in development by rustforms

Explore

Experience audiovisual wonders as you roam through the garden, switching between dimensions to uncover puzzles and discover lurking creatures

Solve

Challenge your mind as you solve spatial puzzles which test your knowledge of crossing dimensions and the differences contained within each

Reveal

Uncover the origins of the garden and its creators from each timeline, and see how your actions have changed the balance between timelines
